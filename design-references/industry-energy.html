Critical Challenges in Modern Energy Operations

The energy sector faces unprecedented pressure to modernize. From aging infrastructure to rigorous compliance standards, operational bottlenecks can lead to costly downtime and security vulnerabilities.

"We understand that in the energy sector, 99.9% uptime isn't a goal—it's the bare minimum requirement for operation."

  • Legacy Infrastructure Integration

    Connecting decades-old SCADA systems with modern cloud IoT platforms without disrupting service.

  • Strict Regulatory Compliance

    Navigating complex NERC-CIP, FERC, and NIST cybersecurity frameworks for connected devices.

  • Unplanned Asset Downtime

    Reactive maintenance leading to expensive truck rolls and lost generation capacity.

  • Data Silos & Interoperability

    Fragmented data sources across distributed sites preventing unified visibility.

  • Edge Connectivity Issues

    Maintaining reliable data transmission in remote areas with unstable network coverage.

Common Questions

How do you handle data security in cloud environments?

We implement a defense-in-depth strategy including encryption at rest and in transit, private VPCs, strict IAM policies, and regular third-party penetration testing. We comply with industry standards like SOC 2 and ISO 27001.

Can your solutions integrate with our existing SCADA systems?

Yes, we specialize in brownfield deployments. Our gateways support standard industrial protocols (Modbus, BACnet, DNP3, OPC-UA) to extract data from legacy PLCs and SCADA systems without replacing them.

What is the typical timeline for a pilot project?

A typical pilot project, from requirement gathering to live deployment, takes between 6 to 10 weeks, depending on hardware availability and integration complexity.

Do you offer 24/7 support for critical infrastructure?

Yes, we offer tiered support packages including 24/7 monitoring and incident response SLAs for mission-critical deployments.

What hardware partners do you work with?

We are hardware agnostic but maintain partnerships with leading vendors like STMicroelectronics, Nordic Semiconductor, and Sierra Wireless to ensure component availability and quality.
