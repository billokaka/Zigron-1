Frequently Asked Questions

We use edge gateways that can speak various industrial protocols (Modbus, BACnet, Profinet) and translate that data into a unified format for our platform.
What is the typical ROI timeline?
Most clients see initial energy savings within the first 3 months of deployment, with full ROI typically achieved between 9 to 14 months.

Yes, we recommend starting with a 'Discovery & Pilot' phase (4-6 weeks) on a limited scope to validate value before full rollout.
